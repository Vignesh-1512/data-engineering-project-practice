
# BR-7: Customer Satisfaction & Reviews

## Business Objective
Analyze customer reviews and satisfaction levels by correlating
review scores with products and delivery performance.

## Grain
**One row per DAY per PRODUCT**

## Final Output Table
brazillian_e_commerce.final.review_satisfaction

## Key Metrics
- Average review score
- Total, positive, and negative reviews
- Average delivery days

## Use Cases
- Product quality monitoring
- Customer experience improvement
- Delivery impact analysis
- Review trend analysis

## Notes
This Gold-layer aggregate table supports customer satisfaction
and quality-driven business decisions.
